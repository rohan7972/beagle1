The Beagle blog

The beagle is breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much
larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as
beagling. Possessing a great sence of smell and superior tracking instinct, the
beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agriculture
imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent
and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health
problems.
